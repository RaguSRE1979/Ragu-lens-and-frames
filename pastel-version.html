About Me

Hello, I'm Raguraman

Based in Voorhees, New Jersey, I'm a passionate photographer dedicated to capturing the moments that matter most — the tender glance between partners, a child's first laugh, and the warmth of family gathered together.

I believe every family deserves photographs as beautiful and unique as the love they share. My approach is relaxed, personal, and always centered around making you feel comfortable in front of the camera.

Whether it's a newborn session, a family portrait, or a milestone celebration, I bring care and creativity to every frame.
