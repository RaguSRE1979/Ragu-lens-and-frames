About

Hello, I'm Raguraman

Based in Voorhees, New Jersey, I'm a photographer with a deep passion for documenting life's most intimate and joyful moments — from the first days of a newborn's life to the warmth of family gathered together.

My approach is unhurried and personal. I believe the best images emerge when people feel at ease, and I work closely with every family to create that environment — whether in a studio, at your home, or in a location meaningful to you.

Every session is treated with the artistry and attention it deserves, resulting in images you'll return to for decades.
